Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

chimpmemory.com ("we", "the site") provides three free browser-based memory games: the Chimp Test, Sequence Memory, and Number Memory. This page explains what data is and isn't collected when you use the site.

How the games work

All three games run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Game state, scores, and history are stored only in your browser's local storage, on your own device, and are never transmitted to or stored on any server operated by this site. Clearing your browser data or using a different browser/device starts your scores fresh.

Cookies and advertising

This site may display advertisements served by third-party ad networks, such as Google AdSense. These networks may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or generally at aboutads.info.

Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate traffic (e.g. page views, approximate location by country, device type). This data is not linked to your scores or game history.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the site owner via the contact link in the footer.
